Requirements For Hunting in New York

NYS requires a 7 hour course in order to become a registered hunter in the state. The course covers the basics of how fire arms work, hunting saftey practices, and other information needed before going on your first hunt

Once the hunter education course is completed one can apply for a nys hunting license. A regular hunting license only covers White Tail Deer, Bears & small game. Seperate licenses are required for turkey and waterfowl. A Hunter must check state limits for the number of animals that can be harvested as yearly limits are based on current population numbers and wildlife management goals.

A NYS hunting license does not act as a NYS gun license. Anyone looking to hunt with firearms will require a seperate NYs firearms license. However, one does not need a firearm to hunt as there are other options available for instance bows and air rifles are other options for those not looking to use firearms.
